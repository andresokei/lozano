About Me

Hello! I'm Andrés Suárez, a web developer from the beautiful region of Asturias. I love the challenge of creating digital experiences that are not only functional but also enjoyable to use. Whether it's building from scratch or enhancing existing projects, I always aim to make the web a little better, one line of code at a time.

With a strong foundation in WordPress and Laravel, and a genuine passion for continuous learning, I've worked on everything from small business sites to larger web applications. What drives me is delivering solutions that make a real difference, both for users and clients.

When I'm not coding, you'll probably find me surfing or exploring new ways to blend creativity and technology. Let's connect—I'm always excited to turn ideas into reality and make great things happen together!

1 2 3

Sociogram.app is an innovative platform built with Laravel, aimed at providing educators and social researchers with the tools to create and analyze sociograms effectively. This web application facilitates social network analysis, allowing users to visualize interpersonal relationships within groups, such as classrooms or research samples.

Key features of Sociogram.app include an intuitive dashboard for managing groups and students, as well as a range of data visualization options that help users uncover social dynamics. The platform also integrates advanced analytics, offering insights that are valuable for understanding group interactions, peer relationships, and social hierarchies.

Built with a focus on usability and scalability, Sociogram.app is a powerful tool for educators and researchers who need to explore and interpret social connections in an accessible way.

Global Glider is an innovative e-commerce platform designed to cater to surf enthusiasts. Built using PHP and vanilla JavaScript, the platform allows users to search surf spots across the globe and access real-time information about weather and wave conditions, making it an essential tool for surfers planning their next adventure.

The site is fully integrated with PayPal's API, enabling secure and seamless transactions for booking surf trips or purchasing surf-related gear. The combination of real-time data and smooth payment integration provides users with a complete and reliable service for planning their surf experiences.

Global Glider stands out for its minimalist yet powerful approach, offering surfers a one-stop solution for exploring the best surf spots worldwide while ensuring they are equipped with up-to-date conditions
